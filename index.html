💼 FinanceOS
Your personal finance co-pilot. Built to give you clarity on where your money goes, help you crush debt faster, and keep you ahead of your bills - without subscriptions, accounts, or data leaving your device.
🔒 100% Private - Everything stays on your device
Getting Started
🏦
Add Your Accounts
Add checking, savings, and cash accounts with real balances. Set a default account and it auto-selects in every payment modal. Add an APY to savings accounts to track projected interest earned.
"How do I track my bank balance?"
💰
Set Up Cash Flow
Add your income sources and recurring expenses. The app uses these to calculate how much is available for debt payoff each month.
"How does the app know my monthly budget?"
📋
Add Your Bills
Add all recurring bills - rent, subscriptions, loans, credit cards. The app tracks due dates, status, and warns you when you're short on funds.
"How do I track what I owe each month?"
🔁
Auto-Log Recurring Payments
Enable "Auto-log" on any bill or expense and FinanceOS logs the payment and debits your account automatically on the 1st of each month.
"Can I stop manually logging the same things?"
Debt & Credit
📉
Debt Planner
Choose Avalanche (highest APR first) or Snowball (smallest balance first). See your exact payoff date and how much interest you'll save.
"When will I be debt-free?"
📊
Loan Payoff Progress
When adding a loan, enter the original amount borrowed. The Debt Planner shows a progress bar with % paid off, amount paid down, and original loan amount - so you can see how far you've come.
"How much of my car loan have I paid off?"
🎁
Promo / 0% Offer Simulator
Got a 0% balance transfer offer or promotional APR? The simulator calculates the fee, monthly payment needed, and whether it's worth it before you commit.
"Is this balance transfer offer worth the fee?"
⚠️
Promo Purchase Tracker
Track PayPal "Pay in X months" and Apple Card installment purchases. See the retroactive interest bomb if you miss the deadline - before it happens.
"How do I track my PayPal no-interest purchases?"
💳
Split Balance System
Credit cards with active promo purchases show separate revolving and promo balances. The payment modal lets you allocate exactly how much goes where.
"How do I pay down my promo vs regular balance?"
Income & Paychecks
📅
Bi-Weekly Paycheck Calculator
Enter your last paycheck date and FinanceOS calculates exactly which months have 2 vs 3 paychecks. Every projection uses the real count, not an average.
"Some months I get 3 paychecks - does the app know?"
🔢
Income Override
Got overtime or a bonus this month? Edit any income entry and enter the actual amount. It overrides for that month only, then auto-resets next month.
"I earned more than usual this month - how do I update it?"
Insights & Tracking
🚨
Spending Anomaly Detection
Compares this month's spending per category against your 3-month average. Yellow = 30% over, Red = 50%+ over. Unusual spending gets flagged automatically.
"Why is my Food category flagged this month?"
📈
Spending Trends by Category
See how any category has trended month-over-month for up to 12 months. Compare two categories side by side and see the % change between months.
"Has my grocery spending been going up?"
📋
Transaction Notes
Add a note to any purchase or withdrawal for context. Notes appear inline on the transaction row. Use the search bar to find transactions by description or note text.
"How do I remember what a purchase was for?"
🎯
Savings Goals
Set a goal with a target amount and deadline. Link it to a savings account to track progress automatically. See the monthly contribution needed to hit it on time.
"How do I save for a vacation or emergency fund?"
✓
Expense Paid Status
Tap the ○ button next to any expense in Cash Flow to mark it paid for the month. A green ✓ PAID badge appears and the row dims. Resets automatically on the 1st of each month.
"How do I know which expenses I've already paid this month?"
Account Management
🔁
Account Transfers
Move money between accounts without it counting as spending. Transfers debit one account and credit another, log in the transaction history with a → indicator, and are excluded from spending stats. Transfers are editable after the fact.
"How do I move money to my savings account?"
💹
APY Interest Tracking
Add an APY to any checking or savings account. The account card shows the rate and your projected annual interest earned based on the current balance — updates live as your balance changes.
"How much interest will my HYSA earn this year?"
🚖
Asset Depreciation
When adding a vehicle, enter the purchase date, original value, and annual depreciation rate. The asset card shows the estimated current depreciated value and total loss — so your net worth reflects reality, not what you paid.
"How do I track my car losing value over time?"
🏠
Home Equity Tracking
Link a property asset to its mortgage loan. The asset card automatically shows your home value, remaining mortgage, and current equity side by side — updates in real time as you make payments.
"How much equity do I have in my house?"
⚖
Bank Reconciliation
Enter your real bank balance and FinanceOS finds the difference. It scans for likely causes - bills paid but not logged, missing deposits - and lets you log an adjustment.
"My app balance doesn't match my bank - how do I fix it?"
⚡
Quick Balance Correction
Each account card has a correction field at the bottom. Type the real balance and tap Correct - no math required. Fast sync without a full reconciliation.
"My balance is slightly off - quick fix?"
Pro Tips
💡 Start with accounts first. Add your checking account balance before anything else. Every payment modal pre-selects the default account, overdraft warnings only work when balances are set, and the Smart Bill Reminder compares your balance to what's due.
💡 Set a default account. In Net Worth → Accounts, the default account shows a purple DEFAULT badge. Edit any account to change it — all payment modals pre-select it automatically.
💡 Use transfers for savings contributions. Moving money to your emergency fund or HYSA? Use the Transfer tile in Quick Update — it moves the balance correctly and won't inflate your spending stats.
💡 Enter original loan amounts. When adding a car loan, mortgage, or student loan, fill in the Original Loan Amount field. The Debt Planner will show you exactly how much progress you've made paying it down.
💡 Link your mortgage to your home. In Net Worth → Assets, edit a property asset and select your mortgage loan from the dropdown. Your equity updates automatically every time you make a payment.
💡 Mark expenses as paid to stay on top of the month. Each expense row has a ○ button — tap it after you pay. The green ✓ PAID badge makes it easy to see at a glance what's done vs still outstanding for the month.
💡 Schedule loan payments for today to see balance drop immediately. When you schedule a debt payment and set the due date to today, the loan balance reduces on the next app load — no waiting for tomorrow.
💡 Undo any delete. Deleted something by accident? A purple undo bar appears for 8 seconds at the bottom of the screen. Tap it to restore.
💡 Export before big changes. Use Settings → Export to Excel to save a backup before loading demo data or resetting the app.
💡 The Debt Planner sorts 0% promos last on purpose. In Avalanche mode, 0% cards go to the bottom - that's correct. Let them ride free while you attack high-APR debt first.
